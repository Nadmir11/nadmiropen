Tournament Rules

Rules

Governing Rules

  • All USGA rules apply unless explicitly stated otherwise.
  • No putts will be given under any circumstance.
  • No capping of score at double par.

Format & Scoring

The tournament consists of one practice round followed by three official tournament rounds. If a player birdies a hole during the practice round, that birdie carries over into their Day 1 score.

Point System (Per Round)

Finish Points
1st+50
2nd+40
3rd+30
4th+20

Bonus Points

  • Birdies: +5
  • Eagle: +20
  • Chip-in for par or better: +10
  • Closest to the pin (Par 3): +5 (max 2 wins per round) (Green -> Fringe -> Rough -> Bunker)
