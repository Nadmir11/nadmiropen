Tournament Rules

  • 1

    Standard stroke play rules apply throughout the tournament.

  • 2

    All competitors are expected to play with integrity, professionalism, and sportsmanship.

  • 3

    A maximum of four players are permitted per group unless otherwise directed.

  • 4

    Ties for first place will be decided by a sudden-death playoff.

  • 5

    Violation of any of these rules will result in immediate death.
