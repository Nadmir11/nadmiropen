Courses

Eagle Harbor Golf Club

Eagle Harbor Golf Club combines scenic beauty with a demanding championship-style layout. Water hazards are a constant presence, framing fairways and guarding greens, while large landing zones tempt players to be aggressive. The course offers a dynamic mix of risk-reward holes that can swing a round in either direction quickly. Well-manicured conditions and dramatic finishing holes make Eagle Harbor a true test of nerve, especially when the pressure is on late in the round.

King and Bear Golf Course

Co-designed by legends Arnold Palmer and Jack Nicklaus, King & Bear is a premier championship course that blends classic design principles with modern challenge. Wide fairways invite bold drives, but approach shots require precision to navigate elevated greens, deep bunkers, and swirling winds. The course balances beauty and brutality, rewarding confident play while exposing mistakes without mercy. Regal in both name and stature, King & Bear demands a complete game from tee to green

Stillwater Golf and Country Club

Stillwater Golf Course offers a serene, inland setting that emphasizes rhythm, precision, and patience. Surrounded by natural wetlands and native Florida vegetation, the course challenges golfers with narrow landing areas, well-protected greens, and subtle elevation changes uncommon in the region. It’s a thinking player’s course—less about raw power and more about disciplined shot selection. Those who respect the layout will find scoring opportunities, while careless play is quickly punished.

Jacksonville Beach Golf Club

Nestled just minutes from the Atlantic Ocean, Jacksonville Beach Golf Club delivers a classic Florida golf experience with a relaxed coastal edge. The course rewards accuracy and smart course management, with strategically placed bunkers, water hazards, and fast, undulating greens that demand focus on every shot. Ocean breezes frequently come into play, adding an extra layer of difficulty and unpredictability. Approachable yet competitive, this course is a favorite for players who enjoy a traditional layout with seaside character.
