Tournament Rules

Rules

1 Practice Round • 3 Tournament Rounds

Practice Round

If you birdie a hole in the practice round, that birdie carries over into your real Day 1 score.

Point System (Per Round)

Finish Points
1st+50
2nd+40
3rd+30
4th+20

Bonus Points

  • Birdies: +5
  • Eagle: +20
  • Chip in for par or better: +10
  • Closest to the pin: +5 (max 2 wins per round)
Closest to the pin priority: On the green first. If everyone misses the green, priority becomes fringe → rough → bunker, then closest distance.

Prize Pool

$25 per person
